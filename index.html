Popular Destinations

Munnar

Munnar rises as three mountain streams merge - Mudrapuzha, Nallathanni and Kundala. 1,600 m above sea level, this hill station was once the summer resort of the British Government in South India.

Read More

Alappuzha

Affectionately nicknamed the 'Venice of the East' by travellers from across the world, Alappuzha is a district of immense natural beauty. Embraced by the Arabian Sea in the west and a network of lakes.

Read More

Wayanad

Wayanad is a rural district in Kerala state, southwest India. In the east, the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is a lush, forested region with areas of high altitude, home to animals including Asiatic elephants, tigers, leopards and egrets.

Read More

Kovalam

Kovalam is an internationally renowned beach with three adjacent crescent beaches. It has been a favourite haunt of tourists since the 1930s. A massive rocky promontory on the beach has created a beautiful bay of calm waters ideal for sea bathing.

Read More

Unique Experiences

Ayurveda - The Science of Healing

Kerala's unique equable climate, natural abundance of forests with a wealth of herbs and medicinal plants, and the cool monsoon season are best suited for Ayurveda's curative and restorative packages.

Ayurveda Wellness

Ayurveda is not just about curative treatments but also about prevention of diseases and promotion of health. Kerala offers excellent Ayurveda wellness packages that rejuvenate your body and mind.

Panchakarma Therapy

Panchakarma is the ultimate Ayurvedic detoxification program. It is a five-fold treatment for mental and physical well-being that is believed to eliminate toxins from the body and restore the body's natural healing ability.

Houseboat Cruise

Glide through the serene backwaters of Kerala on a traditional houseboat, locally known as 'Kettuvallam'. These floating cottages offer a unique way to experience the rustic village life and breathtaking scenery.

Village Canoe Ride

Explore the narrow canals and waterways in a traditional canoe for an intimate experience of the backwater life. Witness the daily activities of villagers, paddy fields, coconut groves and aquatic birds.

Backwater Resorts

Stay at luxurious resorts nestled along the banks of Kerala's backwaters. Wake up to the sight of fishermen in their canoes, enjoy authentic Kerala cuisine and experience unparalleled tranquility.

Varkala Beach

Famous for its cliffside views and mineral springs, Varkala is both a scenic retreat and spiritual destination.

Kovalam Beach

One of Kerala’s most popular beaches, known for its crescent-shaped coastline and lighthouse views.

Bekal Beach

Home to the iconic Bekal Fort, this beach offers tranquility and history in equal measure.

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Located in Thekkady, Periyar is a rich habitat of elephants, tigers, and exotic birds amidst dense forests.

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

This lush sanctuary is home to leopards, elephants, and a variety of flora and fauna.

Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary

Located in the rain shadow area of the Western Ghats, it's known for endangered giant grizzled squirrels.

Onam

Kerala's biggest harvest festival, celebrated with floral carpets, traditional feasts, and Vallam Kali (boat race).

Theyyam

A ritualistic dance form that blends theatre, music, and religious devotion, popular in North Kerala.

Thrissur Pooram

The most famous temple festival in Kerala, known for its magnificent elephant processions and fireworks.

Things to Do in Kerala

Bamboo Rafting

Experience unique bamboo rafting in Periyar's tiger reserve, spotting wildlife along the way.

Trekking

Explore Kerala's Western Ghats with guided treks to Anamudi, Chembra Peak, and more.

Water Sports

Enjoy kayaking, parasailing, and jet skiing at Kovalam, Varkala, and Bekal beaches.

Kathakali Shows

Witness this classical dance-drama with elaborate costumes and expressive movements.

Theyyam Rituals

Experience this ancient ritualistic performance art form in North Kerala temples.

Cooking Classes

Learn to prepare authentic Kerala dishes like appam, stew, and seafood delicacies.

Silent Valley National Park

Explore one of India's last undisturbed rainforests with unique biodiversity in Palakkad district.

Athirapally Waterfalls

Witness the majestic 'Niagara of India' surrounded by lush green forests.

Mangrove Forests

Take a boat ride through Kerala's unique mangrove ecosystems in Kannur and Ernakulam.

Ayurveda Retreats

Experience authentic Ayurvedic treatments and rejuvenation programs at traditional centers.

Yoga Sessions

Join daily yoga classes on the beach or at dedicated wellness centers across Kerala.

Meditation Retreats

Find inner peace with guided meditation sessions in Kerala's tranquil settings.
